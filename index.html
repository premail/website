An easy-to-use component-based build system for MJML, the email templating language, with Handlebars templating, Sass styles, and extra options.

📩   An introduction to Premail   📩

Powered by MJML

Stop fighting those email client rendering gotchas! Premail builds on MJML so you can write less code and ignore all that ridiculousness.

A configurable build process

MJML intentionally doesn't include a build process. This is that build process, and it's configurable to meet your needs and catch the edge cases.

Organize with components in style

Design and structure probably doesn't change every time you hit send. Keep the style and scaffolding pieces of your emails separate from content and ensure consistency.
